Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common rollout, integration, and pilot questions.

Is this officially integrated with ICE or IET submission portals? ▾

The platform is UK-SPEC aligned and export-ready for application workflows. Direct integrations can be scoped separately.

Can candidates and managers both use the system? ▾

Yes. Candidates log evidence and CPD, while managers and mentors review progression and provide structured feedback.

Can we pilot first before a full rollout? ▾

Absolutely. Most customers start with one business unit and expand after proving improved readiness.

How long does onboarding usually take? ▾

Most pilot teams can be onboarded in 1-2 weeks, depending on number of users and readiness of your current CPD/evidence process.

What does a typical pilot include? ▾

A pilot usually covers one business unit, configured competencies, manager workflows, and reporting so you can validate adoption and outcome improvements quickly.

Do you support data export and portability? ▾

Yes. You can export evidence packs and relevant progression data to support submission prep, internal reporting, and continuity needs.

Can this work alongside our existing tools? ▾

Yes. Many teams run CPDPath alongside existing HR/L&D and project systems during pilot, then formalise integration priorities after proving value.

What support do we get during rollout? ▾

You get onboarding guidance, workflow setup support, and practical rollout advice focused on manager adoption and candidate consistency.
