What engineering directors should measure in CPD programs

CPD performance is not just about participation rates. Directors need leading indicators that predict progression quality and reduce registration risk.

Metric 1: competency evidence completeness

Measure coverage quality across competencies, not just count of entries.

Metric 2: manager and mentor review cadence

Track review frequency, turnaround, and feedback quality consistency.

Metric 3: readiness confidence by team

Use confidence scoring and gap trends to prioritise interventions early.

Metric 4: progression outcomes over time

Connect CPD process quality to progression velocity and retention signals.
