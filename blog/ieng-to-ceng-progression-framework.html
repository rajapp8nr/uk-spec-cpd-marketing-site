IEng to CEng progression framework for employers

Many employers support both IEng and CEng pathways, but progression planning is often inconsistent. This framework helps teams create predictable progression with clear evidence and review checkpoints.

✨ AI Summary

  • Stage 1: Baseline and candidate profiling
  • Stage 2: Targeted CPD and experience planning
  • Stage 3: Structured evidence capture

Stage 1: Baseline and candidate profiling

Define current competency coverage, project exposure, and CPD activity patterns for each candidate.

Stage 2: Targeted CPD and experience planning

Set quarterly development goals tied to competency gaps, with practical project assignments where possible.

Stage 3: Structured evidence capture

Capture evidence as work happens, mapping outcomes and personal contribution to competency expectations.

Stage 4: Manager and mentor checkpoint reviews

Use monthly and quarterly checkpoints to maintain momentum and resolve blockers early.

Stage 5: Submission readiness and final preparation

Before submission cycles, confirm evidence completeness, consistency, and confidence across all required areas.
