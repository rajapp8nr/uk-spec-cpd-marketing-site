Best spreadsheet alternative for engineering CPD tracking

Spreadsheets are a common starting point for CPD management, but they often fail once teams scale. Here’s how to evaluate a better workflow without adding unnecessary complexity.

Where spreadsheets break down

  • Inconsistent formatting across teams
  • Poor audit trail for manager sign-off
  • No clear readiness visibility
  • High admin burden near submission windows
  • What is CPD? A practical guide for engineering teams

What to look for in a replacement

  • Structured CPD hour logging and outcome capture
  • Competency-linked evidence workflows
  • Manager/mentor review process with status visibility
  • Export-ready packs and leadership reporting

Low-risk migration approach

Start with one business unit, import active candidates, define review cadence, and benchmark readiness outcomes before scaling.

Pilot KPIs worth measuring

  • Evidence completeness by competency area
  • Manager review turnaround time
  • Candidate readiness confidence score
  • Reduction in last-minute evidence gaps
