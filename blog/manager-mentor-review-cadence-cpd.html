Manager and mentor review cadence for better CPD outcomes

Even strong candidates stall when review cadence is inconsistent. This model helps employers create predictable, useful review cycles that improve evidence quality and progression confidence.

Why cadence matters more than occasional deep reviews

Regular short reviews maintain momentum and reduce end-stage remediation work.

Suggested cadence model

  • Monthly: 20-30 minute candidate review
  • Quarterly: readiness and gap analysis checkpoint
  • Biannual: progression and role exposure planning
  • What is CPD? A practical guide for engineering teams

Feedback quality checklist

  • Specific evidence improvement request
  • Clear next action and owner
  • Timeline for follow-up

Escalation rules for stalled candidates

Define escalation triggers (e.g., no review completion in 6 weeks) and intervention steps to protect progression momentum.
