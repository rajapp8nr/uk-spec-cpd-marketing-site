CEng readiness checklist for engineering employers

Use this checklist to assess whether candidates are genuinely ready for CEng progression — not just active in CPD. The goal is confidence, consistency, and fewer submission surprises.

✨ AI Summary

  • Checklist area 1: CPD activity quality
  • Checklist area 2: UK-SPEC competency evidence
  • Checklist area 3: Manager and mentor review cadence

Checklist area 1: CPD activity quality

  • CPD entries include date, activity, hours, and learning outcome
  • CPD reflects role-relevant development, not generic activity volume
  • Learning outcomes are tied to improved professional practice
  • What is CPD? A practical guide for engineering teams

Checklist area 2: UK-SPEC competency evidence

  • Evidence mapped across competencies A–E
  • Examples show personal contribution and decision ownership
  • Evidence includes measurable outcomes and reflection

Checklist area 3: Manager and mentor review cadence

  • Monthly candidate check-ins are occurring consistently
  • Feedback is specific, actionable, and recorded
  • Escalation path exists for stalled progression

Checklist area 4: Readiness confidence by candidate

Track a practical readiness score with clear next actions. Candidates should know exactly what evidence or development is needed next.
